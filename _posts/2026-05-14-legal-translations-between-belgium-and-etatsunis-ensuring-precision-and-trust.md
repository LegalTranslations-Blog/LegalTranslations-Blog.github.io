## Why Precision Is Paramount in Legal Translations

When it comes to legal translations, every word matters. A single misinterpretation or ambiguous phrase can lead to disputes, non-compliance, or even the invalidation of documents. This is particularly true in multilingual communication involving the Etats-Unis, where legal terminology and procedures can differ greatly from Belgian standards.

- **International contracts** require unequivocal clarity to avoid costly legal battles.
- **Notarial deeds** and official documents must retain their legal force across jurisdictions.
- **Legalizations & apostilles** depend on accurate, consistent translation for recognition by authorities in both Belgium and the Etats-Unis.

## The Role of Sworn and Certified Legal Translators

Quality over speed is a guiding principle in legal document translation. Sworn translations—performed by officially authorized translators—provide an extra layer of trust and legality. Certified legal translators ensure that your documents are not only linguistically accurate, but also comply with the legal requirements of both Belgium and the Etats-Unis.

- **Sworn translations** are essential for court documents, immigration papers, and notarial deeds.
- **Certified legal translators** deliver translations that are recognized by courts, public institutions, and government agencies.

## The Risks of Mistranslations in Legal Contexts

Misinterpretations in international contracts or legal documents can result in:

- Contractual disputes and litigation
- Regulatory non-compliance
- Loss of business opportunities
- Exposure to legal and financial risks
