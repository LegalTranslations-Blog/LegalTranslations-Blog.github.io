Legal documents are precise instruments. A single misinterpretation in an international contract or a notarial deed can lead to disputes, financial loss, or non-compliance. Sworn translations ensure every clause, term, and condition is rendered faithfully, preserving the original intent and upholding legal validity. 

**Key risks of inaccurate legal translations include:**
- Misinterpretations in international contracts, leading to unenforceable agreements
- Compliance violations and regulatory penalties
- Unintended legal consequences due to ambiguous wording
- Increased litigation and reputational damage
