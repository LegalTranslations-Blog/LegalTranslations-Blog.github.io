## Why Accuracy Matters in Legal Translations for Aviation in Belgium

In aviation, every document—from international contracts and notarial deeds to official documents and compliance certificates—must be translated with absolute precision. A single mistranslation or misinterpretation in an international contract can lead to:

- Costly litigation  
- Regulatory penalties  
- Loss of trust and reputation  
- Operational delays  
- Contractual ambiguities

For Brussels Airlines and industry counterparts in Belgium, the need for error correction and quality over speed is paramount. Legal translations must be undertaken by certified legal translators who understand the intricacies of international law and aviation regulations.

## The Role of Sworn and Certified Legal Translators in Belgium

Legal translations require more than linguistic expertise—they demand sworn translators who are legally authorized to certify that translations are faithful and complete. These professionals are essential for handling:

- Sworn translations for court submissions  
- Certified translations of compliance documents  
- Legalizations and apostilles for international use  
- Translation strategy for ongoing multilingual communication

Certified legal translators ensure that every document holds up in court, in compliance reviews, and under scrutiny from regulatory bodies.

## Avoiding Risks and Misinterpretations in International Contracts for Airlines

Misinterpretations in international contracts can have significant consequences, especially for airlines navigating cross-border agreements and aviation law. Common risks include:

- Ambiguous contract clauses leading to disputes  
- Non-compliance with local regulations  
- Invalid notarial deeds due to translation errors  
- Loss of enforceability of official documents

Choosing a translation agency that specializes in legal translation services in Belgium is the first step towards translations without legal risks.
