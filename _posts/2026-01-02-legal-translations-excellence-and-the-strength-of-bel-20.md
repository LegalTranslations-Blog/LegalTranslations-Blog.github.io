## Why Precision in Legal Translations Mirrors bel 20 Standards

Legal translations are not just about converting words from one language to another. They are about safeguarding rights, upholding obligations, and ensuring that multilingual communication is flawless. The companies listed on bel 20 are held to rigorous standards, and so too must your legal translation agency. Here’s why:

- **Accuracy is Non-Negotiable:**  
  Legal documents such as contracts, statutes, and court filings require precise language. A single mistranslation can change the meaning of a clause, leading to misinterpretations in international contracts or compliance failures.

- **Compliance and Legalizations:**  
  Global transactions often require legalizations and apostilles. Only a sworn translation, provided by certified legal translators, meets the requirements of courts and government agencies in Belgium and abroad.

- **Confidentiality Above All:**  
  Legal information is sensitive. Trust & reliability in your translation partner means robust confidentiality protocols, ensuring your data stays protected.

## The Risks of Mistranslations in Legal Contexts

Entrusting legal translations to general translators or automated tools introduces significant dangers:

- **Legal Risks:**  
  Inaccurate legal document translation can lead to unenforceable contracts, disputes, or even financial penalties.

- **Error Correction Costs:**  
  Fixing translation mistakes after the fact is often costly, not only in legal fees but also in reputational damage.

- **International Compliance Issues:**  
  Misinterpretations can result in non-compliance with local or international regulations, putting your organization at risk.

## The Role of Sworn and Certified Legal Translators

Sworn translations are performed by translators accredited by Belgian courts, ensuring every document is officially recognized. These experts have:

- In-depth legal knowledge and linguistic skills  
- Authority to certify translations as true and accurate  
- Experience with complex documents like notarial deeds, contracts, and court judgments

Choosing certified legal translators guarantees that your documents hold up under scrutiny, whether in court or during regulatory audits.

- **Transparency and Trust:**  
  A reputable agency is clear about its processes, pricing, and confidentiality commitments.

- **Proven Track Record:**  
  Choose a translation agency with experience supporting law firms, corporations, and public institutions across Belgium.
