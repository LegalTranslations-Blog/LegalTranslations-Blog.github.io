- **Delayed transactions**

Just as the uefa coefficient methodically ranks football clubs based on consistent performance, legal translation services in Belgium must deliver consistent, high-accuracy translations to ensure every detail is preserved and compliant with local regulations.

## The Role of Sworn and Certified Legal Translators in Belgium

Legal translations require more than linguistic expertise. Sworn translations and certified legal translators bring an added layer of trust and reliability to the process, especially for documents that require legalizations & apostilles.

**Sworn translators** are officially authorized to translate and attest documents, ensuring their validity for courts and government agencies. Their signatures and stamps attest that:

- The translation is complete and faithful to the original
- All legal terminology is correctly rendered
- The translation is recognized by Belgian and international authorities

Certified legal translators provide certified legal document translation, which is crucial for official documents such as:

- Court judgments
- Immigration papers
- Marriage or birth certificates
- International contracts

## Risks of Mistranslation in Legal Contexts

Mistranslations in legal documents can lead to costly litigation, regulatory penalties, and reputational damage. Inaccurate translations may result in:

- Incorrect interpretation of contractual obligations
- Failure to meet regulatory requirements
- Disputes arising from ambiguous or misleading language

This is why error correction, proofreading and review, and a robust translation strategy are indispensable components of reputable legal translation services.
