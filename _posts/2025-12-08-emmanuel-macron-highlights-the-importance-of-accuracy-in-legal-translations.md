## Why Accuracy and Compliance Matter in Legal Translations

Legal translations are not ordinary translations. Every word, clause, and definition can have significant implications—especially in multilingual communication. Misinterpretations in international contracts can lead to costly disputes, compliance issues, or even invalidate crucial agreements. For law firms, corporate legal departments, notaries, and public institutions, the stakes are simply too high to risk errors or ambiguities.

### Risks of Mistranslations in Legal Contexts

- **Legal risks:** An inaccurate translation can result in unintentional breaches of contract or misrepresentation in court.
- **Compliance failures:** Regulatory requirements in Belgium and the EU demand that official documents, legalizations & apostilles, and notarial deeds are translated with utmost precision.
- **Financial consequences:** Misinterpretations can lead to litigation, fines, or loss of business opportunities.

## The Role of Sworn and Certified Translators

Sworn translations and certified legal translators are indispensable for legal professionals. These experts ensure that translations of official documents—such as international contracts, court decisions, or compliance reports—stand up to scrutiny in any jurisdiction. Certified legal translators provide:

- **Accuracy:** Deep understanding of legal terminology and local legal systems.
- **Confidentiality:** Adherence to strict ethical standards to protect sensitive information.
- **Reliability:** Official certifications that add legal validity to translated documents.
