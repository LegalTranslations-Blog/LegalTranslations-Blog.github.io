## Why Accuracy Matters in Legal Document Translation

Legal documents—whether international contracts, notarial deeds, or official documents—demand absolute precision. A single mistranslation in a contract clause or compliance statement can change legal obligations, affect business outcomes, or undermine regulatory filings. In the context of ASML stock, for example, international agreements and cross-border transactions require clarity in every language to ensure enforceability and trust among stakeholders.

### Key Risks of Mistranslations

- **Misinterpretations in international contracts:** Even minor errors can create loopholes, trigger disputes, or cause deals to fall through.
- **Legalizations and apostilles:** Inaccuracies can lead to rejection by courts or government agencies.
- **Compliance failures:** Regulatory missteps due to poor translation can result in fines or loss of operating licenses.
- **Reputation damage:** Trust and reliability are essential in legal matters; a single translation mistake can harm your standing.

## The Role of Sworn and Certified Legal Translators

Sworn translations and certified legal translators are essential for documents intended for official and legal use. In Belgium, certain documents—such as notarial deeds, court decisions, and immigration paperwork—must be translated by sworn professionals whose work is recognized by authorities. This guarantees not only linguistic accuracy but also legal validity.

### When You Need Sworn or Certified Translations

- **Court submissions**
- **International business contracts**
- **Notarial documents**
- **Documents requiring legalizations or apostilles**

These specialists understand legal terminology, jurisdictional nuances, and the requirements of different administrative bodies. Their expertise ensures your translated documents hold up under scrutiny, wherever they are presented.

## Protecting Your Interests Through Confidentiality

Handling sensitive legal information requires more than linguistic skill—it demands an unwavering commitment to confidentiality. A trustworthy translation agency implements strict protocols to safeguard your documents, from secure digital transfers to non-disclosure agreements with every team member.
