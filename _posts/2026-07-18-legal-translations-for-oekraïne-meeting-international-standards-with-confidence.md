## The Role Of Sworn And Certified Translators in Belgian Legal Translation Services

Sworn translations are often a legal requirement in cross-border matters involving official documents such as birth certificates, court orders, or powers of attorney. In Belgium, only sworn translators can provide legally valid translations for these purposes.

Certified legal translators not only translate, but also attest to the accuracy and completeness of the document. This certification is critical for legalizations, apostilles, and any process where the authenticity of the translation may be scrutinised by authorities in België or Oekraïne.
