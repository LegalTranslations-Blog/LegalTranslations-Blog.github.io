## Why Accuracy in Legal Translations Cannot Be Compromised in Belgium

Legal documents are exact by nature. A single mistranslation can lead to misinterpretations in international contracts, regulatory setbacks, or even jeopardize entire legal proceedings. In Belgium, where multilingual legal communication is essential for business, government, and cross-border relations, the significance of sworn translations and certified legal translators cannot be overstated.

### Risks of Mistranslations in Legal Contexts

- **Contractual Disputes:** Ambiguities or errors in translated contracts can trigger lengthy legal battles or void agreements.
- **Compliance Failures:** Incomplete or inaccurate translations may result in non-compliance with Belgian or international regulations.
- **Legalizations & Apostilles:** Mistakes in documents requiring legalizations or apostilles can halt international business or immigration processes.
- **Loss of Confidentiality:** Mishandling sensitive content undermines trust and may breach legal or ethical obligations.

## Sworn and Certified Translators Safeguard Your Interests in Belgium

Not all translations are created equal. Sworn translators and certified legal translators have the expertise and authority to deliver legally valid translations that stand up in court and comply with regulatory requirements. Their work is recognized by Belgian notaries, courts, and government bodies, ensuring that:
- Every term is precisely rendered
- Confidentiality is maintained at every stage
- The translation is legally admissible and certified
