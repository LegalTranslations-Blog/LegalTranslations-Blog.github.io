Legal documents often require sworn translations, meaning they are completed by officially recognized translators whose work is accepted by courts and government agencies. Certified legal translators understand both the linguistic nuances and the strict legal context of each document, ensuring:

- **Faithful reproduction** of the original meaning
- **Compliance with Belgian and EU standards**
- **Confidential handling** of sensitive materials

STIB and similar organizations cannot afford errors in contracts or notarial deeds. Relying on a translation agency with proven expertise in legal translations, error correction, and proofreading and review is crucial.
