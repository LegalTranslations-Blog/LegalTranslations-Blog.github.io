## Why Accuracy in Legal Translations Cannot Be Compromised

Legal documents—whether contracts, court filings, or compliance records—depend on every word holding precise meaning. A single mistranslation can have far-reaching consequences, from invalidating agreements to triggering disputes or regulatory penalties. For law firms and corporate legal departments, particularly during times like the grève 12 mai transports, ensuring that translations remain accurate is not just a matter of efficiency, but of legal necessity.

**Key risks of inaccurate legal translations:**
- Misinterpretations in international contracts
- Non-compliance with local or EU regulations
- Delays in legalizations & apostilles
- Legal liability and financial loss

## The Role of Sworn and Certified Legal Translators

Sworn translations and certified legal translators are essential in Belgium's multilingual legal environment. Sworn translators are officially authorized to provide translations of official documents, such as notarial deeds, birth certificates, and court decisions, that are recognized by public authorities and courts.

### Why choose sworn or certified translators?
- **Legal validity:** Only sworn translations are accepted for many official and court-related procedures.
- **Quality over speed:** Certified legal translators prioritize accuracy and trust & reliability over rushed timelines.
- **Confidentiality:** Professional translation agencies uphold strict confidentiality standards for all legal translations.
