## The Role of Sworn and Certified Legal Translators in Belgium

Sworn translations are not simply literal renderings—they are official translations, performed by certified legal translators who understand the nuances of both source and target legal systems. These professionals ensure:

- Authenticity for official documents and notarial deeds  
- Full compliance with legal requirements for court use  
- Seamless legalizations & apostilles for cross-border recognition  
- Thorough proofreading and review to catch potential errors  

Their expertise is essential in preventing misinterpretations in international contracts and safeguarding your legal standing.
