## Why Accuracy Matters in KMI Legal Translations in Belgium

Every legal document—from notarial deeds and international contracts to compliance paperwork and KMI records—carries significant legal weight. Even a minor error or misinterpretation can:

- Jeopardize court proceedings  
- Invalidate contracts  
- Expose organizations to compliance breaches  
- Trigger costly legal disputes  

For the Koninklijk Meteorologisch Instituut (KMI) and other Belgian institutions, the stakes are even higher when documents cross language borders. Precise multilingual communication is not just a preference—it is a legal necessity in Belgium’s complex legal environment.

## The Role of Sworn and Certified Legal Translators in Belgium

Sworn translations and certified legal translators are central to high-accuracy legal document translation. Only sworn translators, officially recognized by Belgian courts, can provide translations that are legally valid for use in judicial and administrative settings. Their work is:

- **Certified:** Accompanied by an official declaration of accuracy and completeness  
- **Recognized:** Accepted by courts, notaries, and government agencies in Belgium  
- **Confidential:** Bound by codes of professional secrecy  

This ensures that official documents, such as KMI reports or notarial deeds, retain their validity and authority in any language.

## Risks of Mistranslations in Legal Contexts

Legal language is precise, and mistranslations can have wide-reaching implications:

- **Misinterpretations in international contracts** may lead to disputes or unenforceable agreements  
- **Errors in official documents** can cause administrative delays or rejections  
- **Compliance failures** may result in fines or regulatory action  

A translation agency with a proven track record in error correction, proofreading and review, and legalizations & apostilles eliminates these risks, delivering translations without legal risks.
