## The Critical Role of Sworn and Certified Translators

Legal translations demand more than language skills—they require sworn or certified legal translators who understand the nuances of legal systems. In Belgium, sworn translators are authorized by courts to provide certified translations of official documents, such as birth certificates, marriage licenses, or contracts.

What sets these professionals apart?

- **Legalizations & apostilles:** Sworn translators ensure documents are properly legalized for use in foreign jurisdictions.
- **Proofreading and review:** Every translation undergoes meticulous error correction to eliminate potential misinterpretations in international contracts.
- **Confidentiality:** Certified legal translators are bound by strict codes of conduct, ensuring sensitive information remains protected.
