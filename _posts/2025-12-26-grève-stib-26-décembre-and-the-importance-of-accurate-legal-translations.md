## Why Accuracy Matters in Legal Translations in Belgium

Legal translations are not just about converting words from one language to another; they are about conveying intent, rights, and obligations without ambiguity. Each term in international contracts, notarial deeds, and official documents must withstand scrutiny in court or under regulatory review within Belgium and beyond. Errors or misinterpretations can result in costly disputes, compliance failures, or even legal liabilities.

### The Risks of Mistranslations in Legal Contexts

- **Misinterpretations in international contracts** can lead to breaches, financial penalties, or lost business opportunities.
- **Inaccurate legal document translation** of notarial deeds or filings may render documents invalid or unenforceable.
- **Omissions or errors** in official documents can compromise immigration cases, compliance checks, or government tenders.
- Even minor mistakes can escalate into disputes that undermine trust and reliability between parties.

## The Role of Sworn and Certified Legal Translators in Belgium

To protect your interests, legal translations should only be entrusted to sworn translators or certified legal translators authorized in Belgium. These professionals are authorized to provide translations that carry legal validity in Belgium and internationally. Their work is not only accurate but also confidential, ensuring sensitive information is handled according to strict protocols.

Key qualities of sworn and certified translators include:

- **Specialized legal expertise:** Understanding complex legal terminology and structures.
- **Confidentiality:** Adhering to ethical standards to protect client information.
- **Certification:** Providing translations that are recognized by courts, notaries, and public institutions.
