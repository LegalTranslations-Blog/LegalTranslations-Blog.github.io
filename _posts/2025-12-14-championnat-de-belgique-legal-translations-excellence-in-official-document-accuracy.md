## The Role of Sworn and Certified Legal Translators in Belgium

In Belgium, **sworn translations** and **certified legal translators** play a pivotal role. Sworn translators are authorised by the courts to provide translations of notarial deeds, official documents, and other materials that require legal validity. Their seal and signature confirm that the translation is full and faithful to the original, which is critical for:

- **Legalizations and apostilles** for documents destined for international use  
- **Proofreading and review** to eliminate errors  
- **Translations without legal risks**—ensuring documents will be accepted by Belgian public authorities, courts, and regulatory bodies
