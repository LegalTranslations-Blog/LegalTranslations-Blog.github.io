## Why Accuracy Matters in Legal Translations

Legal documents are written to be unambiguous. A single mistranslation can alter the meaning of a clause, shift liability, or render a contract unenforceable. This is why legal translations must be carried out by sworn translators or certified legal translators with expertise in both source and target legal systems.

### The Risks of Misinterpretations in International Contracts

Mistranslations aren’t just typographical errors—they can cause severe legal and financial repercussions:
- **Breach of contract** due to misunderstood obligations
- **Regulatory non-compliance** leading to penalties or lost licenses
- **Disputes in court** over ambiguous or incorrect terms

At Legal Translations, we prioritize quality over speed, ensuring that every translated word holds up under scrutiny and supports our clients’ legal standing.

## Compliance and Confidentiality: Non-Negotiables

Legal translation services demand more than linguistic skill. Compliance with local and international regulations is crucial, especially for documents requiring legalizations or apostilles. Certified legal translators provide sworn translations that meet the standards of Belgian courts and public institutions.

Confidentiality is equally critical. Legal documents often contain sensitive information. A trustworthy translation agency should have robust data protection protocols, guaranteeing the privacy of both clients and subjects.

## The Role of Sworn Translators and Certified Legal Translators

Sworn translators in Belgium are appointed by the courts and authorized to deliver translations of official documents, notarial deeds, and court papers that are accepted by authorities. Certified legal translators bring specialized knowledge and ensure that translations are legally valid and precise.
