## The Role of Sworn and Certified Translators

For many legal procedures in Belgium, including those involving a creche bruxelles, sworn translations are mandatory. These are translations completed and certified by sworn translators recognized by Belgian courts or authorities. Certified legal translators ensure that your documents, such as notarial deeds and official registrations, are accepted by the relevant authorities without question.

The benefits of using sworn and certified translators include:

- **Legally valid translations** for use in courts and before notaries

- **Reduced risk of errors and subsequent legal complications**
