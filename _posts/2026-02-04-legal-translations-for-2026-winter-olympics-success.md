## The Role of Sworn and Certified Translators

A sworn translation, often required for notarial deeds or official documents, is performed by a translator who is legally authorized to provide certified legal translations. This guarantees the document’s authenticity and legal standing in Belgium and abroad. In Olympic contexts, where contracts and legalizations must be recognized across multiple jurisdictions, only certified legal translators can provide the trust and reliability stakeholders expect.
