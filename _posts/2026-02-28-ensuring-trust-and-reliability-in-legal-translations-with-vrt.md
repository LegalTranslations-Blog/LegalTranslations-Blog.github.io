### The Stakes of Mistranslation in Legal Documents

Risks associated with errors in legal translations include:

- **Invalid contracts** due to ambiguous terms or incorrect wording
- **Loss of trust and reputation** for law firms and businesses in Belgium
- **Legal liabilities** and potential court challenges
- **Compliance breaches** with Belgian and EU regulations

A single mistranslation in a notarial deed or official document can have consequences that extend far beyond financial loss—risking the very outcome of legal proceedings.

## The Role of Sworn and Certified Legal Translators in Belgium

Sworn translations and certified legal translators play a central role in safeguarding the integrity of legal documents. In Belgium, a sworn translator is authorized by the court to provide translations that are legally valid for official use. These professionals are bound by strict standards of accuracy, confidentiality, and impartiality.

Key benefits of working with sworn or certified translators include:

- **Legally recognized translations** for use in Belgian courts, notary offices, and government agencies
- **Adherence to compliance requirements** for international and local law
- **Assurance of confidentiality** in handling sensitive information
