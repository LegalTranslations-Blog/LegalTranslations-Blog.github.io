## The Essential Role of Sworn and Certified Translators

For official documents—such as notarial deeds, contracts, or legalizations & apostilles—Belgian authorities typically require sworn translations. These are completed by translators officially recognised by the courts, guaranteeing:

- **Authenticity**: Sworn translations carry legal weight and are accepted by courts, notaries, and government bodies.
- **Reliability**: Certified legal translators ensure that every clause, term, and nuance is accurately rendered, leaving no room for ambiguity.

When dealing with international contracts or immigration procedures, only sworn or certified translations can provide the confidence and legal standing required.
