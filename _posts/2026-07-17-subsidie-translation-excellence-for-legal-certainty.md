## The Role of Sworn and Certified Translators for Legal Translations in Belgium

For legal documents related to subsidie, employing sworn translations is often mandatory. Sworn or certified legal translators are officially authorized to translate legal documents such as:

- International contracts
- Notarial deeds
- Official documents requiring legalizations & apostilles

Their certification ensures:

- **Legal validity** of translations in Belgium and internationally
- **Trust & reliability** for law firms, public institutions, and businesses
- **Translations without legal risks**—essential for subsidie procedures
