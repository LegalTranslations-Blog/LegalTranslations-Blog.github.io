## Why Accuracy Matters in Legal Translations

Legal documents are binding. A single mistranslation can lead to contract disputes, regulatory issues, or even court cases. For law firms, corporate legal departments, and notaries in Herzele, entrusting legal translations to certified legal translators ensures that every nuance is preserved.

**Key reasons accuracy is non-negotiable:**
- **International contracts:** Minor errors can alter obligations or expose parties to unforeseen risks.
- **Notarial deeds:** Legalizations & apostilles require flawless translation to be recognized by foreign authorities.
- **Official documents:** Immigration, compliance, and court submissions demand certified, error-free translations.

## The Role of Sworn and Certified Translators

Not all translators are equipped for legal work. Sworn translators, recognized by Belgian authorities, provide certified legal translations that hold up in court and before notaries. Their expertise guarantees:
- **Confidentiality:** Sensitive information stays protected.
- **Legal validity:** Sworn translations meet the stringent standards required for legalizations, apostilles, and official filings.
- **Error correction:** Rigorous proofreading and review processes minimize legal risks.
